## Day 93 – Advanced Joins & Subqueries (Conceptual Explanation)

This level focuses on solving
complex SQL problems
using joins and subqueries.

---

### Key Concepts Covered

1. Correlated subqueries
2. Nested queries
3. Aggregation with subqueries
4. Anti-joins (LEFT JOIN + NULL)
5. Business logic queries

---

### Important Patterns

Subquery in WHERE:
- Used for comparison
- Example: salary > average

Correlated Subquery:
- Depends on outer query
- Runs for each row

HAVING with subquery:
- Used for group-level conditions

---

### Advanced Join Usage

LEFT JOIN + NULL:
- Used to find missing records

JOIN with conditions:
- Used for filtering related data
