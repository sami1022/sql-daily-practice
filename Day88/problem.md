## Day 88 – Sales Analysis (Revenue Insights)

### Problem Statement

You are given a table **orders**:

- order_id (INT)
- customer_id (INT)
- product_id (INT)
- amount (DECIMAL)
- order_date (DATE)

Write SQL queries to:

1. Find total revenue per month
2. Identify top 5 customers by revenue
3. Find highest selling day
4. Calculate revenue growth (day-over-day)
5. Find top performing product

Focus on extracting business insights from sales data.
