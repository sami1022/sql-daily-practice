## Day 88 – Sales Analysis (Conceptual Explanation)

Sales analysis helps businesses
understand revenue patterns
and performance.

SQL is widely used
to generate these insights.

---

### Revenue Analysis

Revenue analysis answers:

- How much money is generated?
- When is revenue highest?
- Which customers contribute most?

---

### Time-Based Analysis

Grouping data by time helps:

- track growth
- detect trends
- identify seasonality

Examples:
- daily revenue
- monthly revenue

---

### Customer Analysis

Customer-level metrics help identify:

- top customers
- high-value segments
- repeat buyers

---

### Product Analysis

Product analysis helps find:

- best-selling products
- high-revenue items
- performance comparison

---

### Growth Analysis

Growth metrics measure change over time.

Example:
- day-over-day growth
- month-over-month growth

This is important for business decisions.

---

### Real-World Applications

Sales analysis is used in:

- e-commerce platforms
- retail businesses
- financial reporting
- marketing analytics

---

### Interview Importance

Common interview questions:

- Find top customers
- Calculate revenue trends
- Analyze growth metrics
- Identify best products

---

### Summary

Sales analysis includes:

- revenue calculations
- customer insights
- product performance
- time-based trends

This is core Data Analyst work.
