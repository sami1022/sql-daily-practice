## Day 98 – Final Project (E-commerce SQL Analysis)

### Scenario

You are working as a Data Analyst for an e-commerce company.

You are given:

**orders**
- order_id (INT)
- customer_id (INT)
- product_id (INT)
- amount (DECIMAL)
- order_date (DATE)

**customers**
- customer_id (INT)
- signup_date (DATE)

**products**
- product_id (INT)
- category (VARCHAR)

---

### Business Questions

1. What is total revenue and monthly growth?
2. Who are the top 10 customers?
3. Which product categories generate most revenue?
4. What is customer retention rate?
5. What is average order value (AOV)?
6. Identify inactive customers (no orders in last 60 days)
7. Calculate running revenue trend

Focus on end-to-end business analysis.
