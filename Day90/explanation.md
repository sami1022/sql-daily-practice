## Day 90 – Product Metrics (Conceptual Explanation)

Product metrics help measure
how users interact with a product.

They are essential for
product growth and decision-making.

---

### Daily Active Users (DAU)

DAU measures
how many users
use the product daily.

It indicates:
- engagement
- product usage

---

### Monthly Active Users (MAU)

MAU measures
unique users
over a month.

It shows:
- product reach
- user base size

---

### Churn

Churn refers to users
who stop using the product.

High churn indicates:
- poor retention
- product issues

---

### Retention Rate

Retention measures
how many users return
after first use.

High retention = strong product value

---

### Engagement Metrics

Engagement shows
how frequently users interact.

Examples:
- number of logins
- actions per user

---

### Real-World Applications

Product metrics are used in:

- mobile apps
- SaaS platforms
- e-commerce
- social media platforms

---

### Interview Importance

Common interview questions:

- Calculate DAU/MAU
- Find churn rate
- Calculate retention
- Identify active users

---

### Summary

Product metrics include:

- DAU & MAU
- retention & churn
- engagement metrics

These are core for product analytics.
