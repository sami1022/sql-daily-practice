## Day 92 – Interview SQL (Basic Level)

### Problem Statements

Solve the following SQL interview questions:

---

### Q1. Employees Earning More Than Their Manager
Find employees whose salary is greater than their manager.

---

### Q2. Customers Who Ordered More Than Once
Find customers who placed more than one order.

---

### Q3. Find Missing IDs
Find IDs missing from a sequence.

---

### Q4. Highest Salary in Each Department
Find highest salary per department.

---

### Q5. Count Orders Per Day
Find number of orders placed each day.

Focus on logic building and edge cases.
