## Day 62 – Practice (Set Operation Problems)

### Problem Statement

You are given three tables:

**students_2023**
- student_id (INT)
- name (VARCHAR)

**students_2024**
- student_id (INT)
- name (VARCHAR)

**dropouts_2024**
- student_id (INT)
- name (VARCHAR)

Write SQL queries to:

1. Find all students across both years
2. Find students enrolled in both years
3. Find students who enrolled in 2024 but not in 2023
4. Find students who dropped out in 2024
5. Count total unique students across both years

Focus on applying set operations in practical scenarios.
