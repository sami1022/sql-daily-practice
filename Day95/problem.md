## Day 95 – Case Study (Business SQL Problems)

### Scenario: E-commerce Analysis

You are given two tables:

**orders**
- order_id (INT)
- customer_id (INT)
- order_date (DATE)
- amount (DECIMAL)

**customers**
- customer_id (INT)
- signup_date (DATE)

---

### Tasks

1. Calculate monthly revenue
2. Find new customers per month
3. Calculate repeat customer rate
4. Find top 5 customers by total spending
5. Calculate average revenue per user (ARPU)

Focus on solving a real business case.
